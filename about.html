About the Disney Courier

The Disney Courier was created by a very special group of Disney Annual Passholders
and Disney Fans who had a dream for a place on the internet
that people young and old could share their love of Walt Disney
and his legacies in a safe and fun atmosphere.

The Disney Courier is a blog that will be written by several geeks that are on the Disney Courier Magic staff.
This will be about anything Disney that we come up with. It could be about Disney related topics,
but definitely will not be confined to it. So check back and see as we write about Disney movies,
Star Wars, Star Trek, comic books, conventions, events, and whatever else suits our fancy.
